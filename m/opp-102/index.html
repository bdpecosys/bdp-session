Meeting Context

Netflix is one of the world's largest AWS customers, running a globally distributed streaming platform across hundreds of AWS accounts and engineering teams. Their infrastructure generates significant cloud spend, with AWS cost governance and resource efficiency as an active priority for their cloud and security leadership.

Zesty is an AI-powered AWS optimization platform that autonomously manages Reserved Instance commitments, Savings Plans, and resource rightsizing across multi-account AWS environments — without requiring engineering intervention. Zesty's platform is purpose-built and highly-secured for organizations like Netflix, where the scale of accounts and workloads makes manual optimization impractical.

The opportunity: Zesty's autonomous multi-account AWS optimization directly addresses the cost, governance and security challenge Netflix faces at scale — with the potential to generate significant savings across their AWS estate while freeing engineering teams from manual commitment management.
