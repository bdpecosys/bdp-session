Step 4 of 5

Google News × Claude = real buying intent, not keyword noise

The engine fetches news across all signal queries, then Claude reads every article and extracts only the companies showing genuine buying intent — with a confidence score and reason.

  • One Claude call per signal processes up to 30 articles simultaneously
  • Named contacts mentioned in the article are extracted automatically
  • Deduplication prevents the same company appearing twice

ISV Prospector
Step 2 of 4

Claude generates geo-targeted search queries, then reads the news

One Claude call generates 3 targeted queries per signal — every query includes geography and sector keywords. The engine then fetches and processes up to 20 articles per query.

  • ~300 article slots scanned per run (5 signals × 3 queries × 20 articles)
  • Claude extracts the ISV from each article — not the partner, investor, or enterprise
  • Geography is enforced both in the prompt and as a post-processing filter
Total API cost per scan: typically $0.04 – $0.08
